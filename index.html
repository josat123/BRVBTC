💡 How to Buy BRVBTC

Two ways to get BRVBTC – choose what suits you best

🔁 Option 1: Buy on Uniswap (Easiest)

If you already have USDC, WBTC, or ETH, you can swap directly on Uniswap V4.

🌐 On Ethereum L1:

  • ✅ Go to Uniswap App
  • ✅ Select network: Ethereum
  • ✅ Input token: USDC or WBTC
  • ✅ Output token: BRVBTC
  • ✅ Contract address:
    0x9bc0F4d4B31AdEa0c7Fde6f40a778E4Ce7Bc652d

⛓️ On Polygon L2 (cheaper fees):

  • ✅ Switch network to Polygon
  • ✅ Go to Uniswap on Polygon
  • ✅ Swap USDC or posWBTC → BRVBTC
  • ✅ Contract address:
    0xa5c96d77C280B9F4bA13cd4064C4864Cf69a3BCB

💧 Pool liquidity: $105 on L1 • $100 on L2

⚙️ Option 2: Mint with WBTC (Direct)

If you hold WBTC (or posWBTC), you can mint BRVBTC directly from the contract at the mathematical rate: 1 WBTC = 100,000 BRVBTC = $100,000.

📦 Mint on Ethereum L1:

  1. Get WBTC (Wrapped Bitcoin)
  2. Go to contract on Etherscan
  3. Connect wallet (Write Contract tab)
  4. First call approve to allow contract to spend your WBTC
  5. Then call mint(wbtcAmount)

⛓️ Mint on Polygon L2:

  1. Get posWBTC (bridged WBTC on Polygon)
  2. Go to contract on Polygonscan
  3. Same process: approve → mint

📐 Math: 1 WBTC = 100,000 BRVBTC → 1 BRVBTC = 1 USD
