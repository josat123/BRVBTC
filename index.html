💡 How to Buy BRVBTC

Two ways to get BRVBTC – choose what suits you best

🔁 Option 1: Buy on Uniswap (Easiest)

If you already have USDC, WBTC, or ETH, you can swap directly on Uniswap V4.

🌐 On Ethereum L1:

  • ✅ Go to Uniswap App
  • ✅ Select network: Ethereum
  • ✅ Input token: USDC or WBTC
  • ✅ Output token: BRVBTC
  • ✅ Contract address: 0x9bc0F4d4B31AdEa0c7Fde6f40a778E4Ce7Bc652d

⛓️ On Polygon L2 (cheaper fees):

  • ✅ Switch network to Polygon
  • ✅ Go to Uniswap on Polygon
  • ✅ Swap USDC or posWBTC → BRVBTC
  • ✅ Contract address: 0xa5c96d77C280B9F4bA13cd4064C4864Cf69a3BCB
💧 Pool liquidity: $332.74 on L1 • $422.62 on L2

⚙️ Option 2: Mint with WBTC (Direct)

If you hold WBTC (or posWBTC), you can mint BRVBTC directly at the mathematical rate: 1 WBTC = 100,000 BRVBTC = $100,000.

📦 Mint on Ethereum L1:

  1. Get WBTC
  2. Go to contract on Etherscan
  3. Connect wallet (Write Contract)
  4. First call approve to allow contract to spend your WBTC
  5. Then call mint(wbtcAmount)

⛓️ Mint on Polygon L2:

  1. Get posWBTC (bridged WBTC on Polygon)
  2. Go to contract on Polygonscan
  3. Same process: approve → mint
📐 Math: 1 WBTC = 100,000 BRVBTC → 1 BRVBTC = 1 USD

About BRVBTC

BRVBTC was built over 4 years of development to create a truly transparent, decentralized stable asset. It combines the security of Bitcoin (via WBTC) with a mathematical peg to 1 USD, enforced by a 50% on-chain reserve. No central control, no hidden fees, no wrapped tokens — just pure, verifiable value.
